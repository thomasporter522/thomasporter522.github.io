NUTELLA 2025

May 5 - 9, 2025

Welcome to the website of the 1st Non-University Trip for Exploring Live Language Applications (NUTELLA 2025). NUTELLA 2025 will take place in Port Sanilac, Michigan.

The annual Non-University Trip for Exploring Live Language Applications is a forum for the discussion of all aspects of the Hazel programming language and environment. Both polished presentations and prospective discussions are welcome on topics ranging from parsing to live GUIs. We seek submissions that make principled, enduring contributions to the theory, design, understanding, implementation, or application of Hazel.

See the Call For Events for detailed information.
