NUTELLA 2025 Call for Events

NUTELLA 2025 seeks contributions on all aspects of Hazel, both theoretical and practical. Authors of Hazel-relate papers will be invited to present their work in the NUTELLA conference in May 2025, as will those conductive ongoing projects.

Scope

Non-University Trip for Exploring Live Language Applications (NUTELLA) is a forum for the discussion of all aspects of Hazel. Both theoretical and experimental projects are welcome, on topics ranging from formal frameworks to experience reports. We seek submissions that make principled, enduring contributions to the theory, design, understanding, implementation, or application of Hazel.

Evaluation Criteria

The Review Committee will evaluate the technical contribution of each submission as well as its accessibility to both experts and the general lab audience. All events will be judged on significance, originality, relevance, correctness, and clarity.
